Obese
Based on your BMI, you can be considered obese. This likely means you carry a large amount of body fat that could eventually cause health problems such as diabetes, heart disease, or high blood pressure down the road. You would likely significantly benefit from a diet change and getting some form of consistent exercise. If you are a bodybuilder, your BMI designation could be incorrect. If not, a visit to your family physician might be helpful for you to better understand what risks you may have.
